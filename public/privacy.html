Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Silent Exif is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within our iOS application.

1. Data Collection & Privacy

No Personal Data Collection: Silent Exif does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, photos, or metadata to any external servers. All photo processing and metadata modification are performed locally on your device.

2. Photo Library Access & Permissions

Purpose: Our app requires "Read and Write" access to your Photo Library solely to allow you to select photos for metadata viewing and to save the modified versions back to your device. Local Only: All access is handled through standard iOS system APIs. No Uploads: We do not transmit, backup, or store your photos on any server. Your images stay on your device at all times.

3. Offline Functionality

Silent Exif is designed to work entirely offline. Since no data is uploaded, your privacy is guaranteed by the security of your own iOS device.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that track your behavior or collect your data.
